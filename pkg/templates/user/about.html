About Us

Naija Unity House is a non-profit organisation that is championing the course of fostering unity among Nigerians and harness the benefit of our rich ethno-religious diversity with focus on the youths. The organisation is duely registered with relevant government agencies in Nigeria. In 2019, the organisation was incooperated with the Coperate Affairs Commission (CAC) and also got its copy right. The organisation aims to achieve the following objectives:

  • To provide a platform for exchange of ideas for the betterment of Nigeria
  • To help in sanitizing youths through organization of Seminars and workshops
  • To give a helping hands to the less privilege
  • To serve as an advisory body in check making the activities of Governance.

Background

Naija Unity Forum, Initiative for forstering unity in Nigeria

Nigeria is a heterogeneous country with sundry diversities in religion, tribes, culture and values. In absence of tolerance, diversities can create suspicion within polity. Despite strides by the federal government to enhance national integration and cohesion, however, unity is still an issue the country is grappling is exemplified by various incidences of sectarian bigotry, discrimination and intolerance which arise from existing disparities in culture, tribe and religion. Sometimes sporadic violence has sprang up from grievances fueled up by mismanagement of diversities with the attendant erosion of peace and harmony.Read more

Our Mission

To explore the tools of entertainment as a platform that reinforces the value of unity in diversity with a view to enhance national cohesion, stability and growth.

Our Vision

It is imperative to create a culture and consciousness for national unity and integration with the right perception that diversities translate to national strength , when tolerance and deference outweighs suspicion and discrimination driven by sectarian bigotry.
